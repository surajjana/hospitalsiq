About

HospitalsIQ is a platform that can be used by any common person suffering from an ailment to locate a hospital for it. Firstly the platform ellicits the location and the ailment of the person. It then compares the information provided with the database. The hospitals matched are then listed down. To sum up a person can find out a hospital nearby for the care required.
